About Us

Rooted in the rich legacy of Indian craftsmanship, Desinaar is a menswear label that celebrates the timeless beauty of ethnic wear. Designed for the modern man with a reverence for tradition, Desinaar brings together regal silhouettes, artisanal detailing, and a deep connection to Indian culture.

Our collections, Riwayat and Rutba, are an ode to the vibrant heritage of India. From classic and intricately embroidered kurtas, each piece reflects the grandeur and grace of festive Indian attire. Inspired by the rituals, colors, and textures of our diverse traditions, this look book captures the essence of celebration and elegance.

At Desinaar, we don't just dress men — we honor legacy, style, and the spirit of festivity.
